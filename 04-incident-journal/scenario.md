# Scenario — Ransomware Attack on a U.S. Healthcare Clinic

## Overview

A small U.S. healthcare clinic specializing in primary-care services experienced a ransomware security incident on **Tuesday, September 9th, 2025, at approximately 9:00 a.m.** The attack resulted in the full encryption of critical patient and operational files, causing a complete shutdown of business operations.

---

## Incident Description

Upon arriving at the clinic that morning, several employees discovered they were unable to access their workstations, medical records, or any of the software required to perform their duties. Shortly after, a **ransom note** appeared on the affected computers, notifying the organization that all files had been encrypted by a threat actor group known for targeting organizations in the **healthcare and transportation industries**.

The attackers demanded a large sum of money in exchange for the decryption key needed to restore access to the encrypted data.

---

## Attack Vector & Execution

The intrusion was carried out through a **targeted phishing campaign** directed at multiple employees within the organization. The phishing emails contained malicious attachments that, once downloaded, installed malware on the compromised endpoints. This gave the attackers initial access to the internal network.

Once inside, the threat actors deployed ransomware that propagated through the network and encrypted critical files — including patient medical records — rendering them completely inaccessible.

---

## Impact

| Area | Impact |
|---|---|
| Patient data | Critical medical records encrypted and inaccessible |
| Business operations | Full operational shutdown |
| IT systems | All affected computer systems taken offline |
| Regulatory exposure | Potential HIPAA violations due to unauthorized access to PHI |

---

## Threat Actor Profile

The attack was attributed to an **organized cybercriminal group** with a documented pattern of targeting high-impact sectors such as healthcare and transportation — industries where operational disruption creates maximum pressure on victims to pay the ransom quickly.

---

## Initial Response

Following the discovery of the incident, the organization was forced to:

- Shut down all computer systems to contain the spread of the ransomware
- Contact relevant authorities and regulatory bodies to report the incident
- Engage external cybersecurity specialists for technical assistance and forensic investigation
